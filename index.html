You are curious how history would sound if it could talk

The "I Am" series explores that question through first person stories.

If the United States Army could tell its own story, what would it say. If gold could explain what people have done for it, how would it sound. If the Great Wall of China could talk about what it has seen, what details would it share.

What makes the series different

The "I Am" series gives voice to cities, armies, elements, places, and people. Each book speaks in first person so the subject explains itself. Readers who want history to feel personal often begin here. The structure makes names, dates, and events easier to remember because they arrive through a single steady voice.

For the reader, this changes the experience from reading about a subject to hearing from it.

Meet Tobe Garrett

Tobe Garrett is an independent author based in Austin, Texas, with more than fifty five titles in science fiction, historical fiction, fantasy, adventure, and how to topics. The thread that runs through the work is careful research, thoughtful storytelling, and practical knowledge that connects with readers.

Each book follows a simple pattern. Research and planning, drafting and revision, and a focus on respect for the reader. The goal is that a reader closes the book with a clearer picture or skill than they had at the start.

How each book comes to life

Research & planning: digging deep into each topic or imagined world before the draft begins.

Drafting & revising: shaping each manuscript through multiple rounds to sharpen clarity, pacing, and emotional depth.

Reader respect: whether fiction or nonfiction, the work aims to reward the reader’s time.
