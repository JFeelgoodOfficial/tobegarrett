The “I Am” Series

What if history, creatures, and even gold leaned in and spoke first?

History, but whispered in your ear

What if historic people and places could speak? The “I Am” Series gives voice to the cities, states, places, and people that shape our world. Each book is written from a first-person perspective, turning history into a personal, engaging, and unforgettable experience.

I'm Tobe Garrett, independent author and book seller

An independent author based in Austin, Texas, I’ve published 55+ titles across science fiction, historical fiction, fantasy, adventure, and how-to guides — available in paperback and e-book on Amazon. Every book reflects my commitment to storytelling and practical knowledge that connects with readers.

I write across genres with depth and purpose. Each book reflects careful research, thoughtful storytelling, and a simple goal: to leave you with something you didn’t have when you opened the cover — a new idea, a new angle on history, or a practical way forward.

How the books come to life

Every title in my catalog goes through careful research, thoughtful drafting, and thorough editing to deliver quality stories and guides.

Research & Planning: I dig deep into each topic or world, gathering details and outlining structure before the first draft begins.

Drafting & Revising: Multiple rounds of writing and refinement shape the manuscript, ensuring pacing, clarity, and emotional depth throughout the narrative.
