The “I Am” series

This is where the positioning separates him from a lot of other authors.

Instead of describing the subject from the outside, these books let the subject tell the story in first person.

What makes the series different

The “I Am” series gives voice to cities, states, places, people, and symbols that shape the world. Each title is written from a first-person perspective, which turns history into something personal, engaging, and easier to remember.

For the reader, that changes the experience from reading about the subject to hearing from it.

About the author

He writes with range, but the thread running through the work is clarity.

That is important when someone is deciding whether this is an author they want to spend time with.

Meet Tobe Garrett

Tobe Garrett is an independent author based in Austin, Texas, with 55+ titles spanning science fiction, historical fiction, fantasy, adventure, and practical how-to guides. The throughline is careful research, thoughtful storytelling, and practical knowledge that connects with readers.

The strongest way to present him is as someone who knows how to take a big subject and make it readable, relatable, and worth finishing.

How each book comes to life

Research & planning: digging deep into each topic or imagined world before the draft begins.

Drafting & revising: shaping each manuscript through multiple rounds to sharpen clarity, pacing, and emotional depth.

Reader respect: whether fiction or nonfiction, the work aims to reward the reader’s time.
